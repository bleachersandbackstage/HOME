About the Publication

Original Reporting. Authentic Photography.

Bleachers & Backstage is an independent editorial publication covering sports, music, and live events across the East Coast through original photography and editorial storytelling.

Founded by Katie Rygiel, the publication is dedicated to covering the moments that happen at field level and in the photo pit — the ones that tell the full story of a game, a show, or a night out on the Shore.
